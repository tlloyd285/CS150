The Quokka

Where to find them:

Quokkas are native to a select few islands off of the Western Coast of Australia, particularly Rottnest Island.

Info:

Quokkas are marsupials, closely related to other Australian Wildlife such as Kangaroos and Wallabies. The Quokka is a herbivore and is mainly nocturnal.

Wiki

"The selfie animal"

Quokkas are famous for their 'smiling' face and friendliness with humans, they enjoy the camera and are very curious. Quokkas also have no natural predators on rottnest island so they are very comfortable with human interaction.
